Data Deletion Request

We respect your privacy and provide a way for you to delete your data from our system.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you want to delete your data associated with our Facebook app, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook App Settings.
  2. Find and select our app from the list.
  3. Click on "Remove" and ensure the option to delete all associated data is selected.

Alternatively, you can submit a manual request by contacting us at [Your Contact Email] with your Facebook User ID.
